Gingersnap Cookies

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

2. Cream the vegan butter and brown sugar together and then add the molasses and vanilla extract.

3. Sift the flour into a bowl and add the baking soda, salt, ground ginger, cinnamon, allspice and cloves and mix together.

4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet and stir in by hand until crumbly.

5. Add the 2 Tbsp non-dairy milk and mix. You should get a very thick cookie dough. However, if you still have crumbles and its not yet sticking together then add another Tbsp non-dairy milk. Only do this if you really need it though.

6. Break off roughly tablespoon sized pieces and roll into balls and then roll the balls in white sugar. If you tend to make larger cookies, then you’ll need to bake them for a few minutes longer (around 15 minutes) to get them nice and crunchy.

7. Place the balls onto a parchment lined baking tray and press down with a fork.

8. You might want to do these in two batches so that your cookies are nicely spaced out on the baking tray.

9. Bake in the oven for 12 minutes. If you want more soft and chewy then bake for 10 minutes, if you want more snap/crunch then bake for 12 minutes. If your cookies are larger in size then bake for up to 15 minutes.

10. The cookies will be soft when they come out the oven. Allow to cool completely as that is when they will firm up and become crunchy.

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup (56g) Vegan Butter
1 cup (200g) Brown Sugar
1/4 cup (75g) Unsulphured Molasses
1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 cups (250g) All Purpose Flour
1 tsp Baking Soda
1/4 tsp Salt
2 tsp Ground Ginger
2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
1/4 tsp Allspice
1/4 tsp Ground Cloves
2-3 Tbsp Non-Dairy Milk
1/4 cup (50g) White Sugar (for rolling)

TIME NEEDED

Prep Time: 25 minutes || Cook Time: 12 minutes
