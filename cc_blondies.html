Chocolate Chip Blondies

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). In a bowl, mix the almond butter, non dairy milk, coconut sugar and vanilla until well combined and smooth.

2. Sift the flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon into the bowl. Or whisk them together in another bowl and mix it.

3. Fold in the chocolate chips. Drop the thick stiff batter into a greased or parchment lined pan. Using a spatula press the batter down to event it out into the pan. Sprinkle a tablespoon or so chocolate chips on top and press in.

4. Bake for 23 to 26 minutes. The top should be set. Let the blondies cool for 15 minutes before slicing and gobbling up. Use heart shaped cookie cutter to cut out shapes.

INGREDIENTS



1/2 cup + 2 tbsp almbond butter or use another nut butter

1/4 cup + 2 tbsp non dairy milk

3/4 cup coconut sugar or other sugar

2 tsp vanilla extract

3/4 cup unbleached white flour

1/4 tsp baking soda

1/4 cup oat flour

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp cinnamon

1/3 to 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

TIME NEEDED

Prep Time: 15 minutes || Cook Time: 25 minutes
