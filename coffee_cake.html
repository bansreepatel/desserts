Coffee Cake

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

2. Make the crumb topping by combine the flour, sugar, salt and cinnamon. Cut in the vegan butter with a fork. Keep pressing the fork into the butter as you stir, until the mixture is fully mixed and a crumbly texture. Set aside.

3. Combine the dry ingredients for the cake

4. Cut in the vegan butter with a fork or pastry blender.

5. Make the flax egg. Add the plant-based milk and vanilla.

6. Add the wet ingredients to the flour mixture and stir just until moistened.

7. Pour into a greased 9x13 inch pan.

8. Bake at 350 degrees of for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.

9. Allow to cool.

INGREDIENTS

CRUMB TOPPING:

1/4 cup flour

2/3 cup sugar

1 teaspoon cinnamon

2 tablespoons vegan butter

pinch of salt

CAKE:

2 cups flour

1/2 cup vegan butter

3/4 cups sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 flax egg or egg replacer

1 cup plant-based milk (soy, almond, oat)

2 teaspoons vanilla

TIME NEEDED

Prep Time: 20 minutes || Cook Time: 25-30 minutes
