Lemon Cake

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease two 8 inch cake pans, line with parchment rounds (I cut them out myself to fit the bottom of the pans, prevents sticking), and grease the parchment paper as well. Sprinkle lightly with flour.

2. In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.

3. In a measuring cup, combine the almond milk and apple cider vinegar. Let sit a few minutes until it curdles (this is your vegan buttermilk).

4. In a small bowl, add the 1/2 cup aquafaba and whisk for a minute or two, until it is a bit foamy. Set aside.

5. In a large bowl, using a standing mixer or handheld mixer, add the sugar and lemon zest. Stir to combine. Now add the vegan butter and beat on medium speed until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Now add in the lemon and vanilla extracts and beat to combine.

6. On medium speed, add in the almond milk mixture and aquafaba. Now add the flour mixture and beat until well combined, about 3-4 minutes.

7. Divide the batter equally in the prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cool on wire racks for 5-10 minutes, then run a knife around the edges to loosen, and carefully invert the cakes onto your hand. Remove the parchment paper and place on a wire rack to cool completely.

8. Make the frosting: Using a standing mixer or handheld mixer, beat the vegan cream cheese and vegan butter together until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes. Reduce the speed, add in the 2 3/4 cups powdered sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest and vanilla extract. Beat on low, then switch to medium speed and beat until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. If it is too runny, add the rest of the powdered sugar, or more as needed.

9. Frost the cake: Place one of the cake layers on your cake stand or plate. Frost the top evenly. Place the second cake layer on top. (If your cakes are not fairly flat, use a large serrated knife to slice off the tops to create a flat surface for frosting) Top the second layer with more frosting, and frost the sides as well. Decorate as desired and serve!

INGREDIENTS

CAKE:
2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cups almond milk
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup aquafaba
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
2 teaspoons freshly grated lemon zest
8 tablespoons vegan butter, softened to room temperature
1 teaspoon pure lemon extract
2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

FROSTING:
8 ounce vegan cream cheese
8 tablespoons vegan butter, softened to room temperature
2 3/4 - 3 cups powdered sugar, sifted
2 tablespoons fresh squeezed lemon juice
1 tablespoon freshly grated lemon zest
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

TIME NEEDED

Prep Time: 20 minutes || Cook Time: 30 minutes || Cooling Time: 1 hour
